Beef Stew and Rice Recipe

BEEF STEW

Ingredients:

* 1 Tablespoon of vegetable oil
* 1 kilogram of diced beef meat
* 1 chopped onion
* 2 peeled and cut carrots
* Freshly ground black pepper
* 3 cloves of minced garlic
* 1/4 tomato paste
* 1/2 kilogram of cut potatoes
* 1/2 kilogram of peas

Directions:

1. In a large sufuria, heat oil over medium heat. Add beef and cook until seared on all sides for ten minutes.Transfer the beef to a plate.
2. Add more oil if necessary, then in the same sufuria, cook onions and carrots until soft for 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Add garlic and tomato paste and cook until garlic is fragrant and darkened for 2 minutes.
4. Add beef back to the sufuria. Bring it to a boil then reduce heat to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and let it simmer until beef is tender for 30 to 45 minutes.
5. Add potatoes and simmer while covered, until potatoes are tender for 15 minutes.
6. Stir in the peas and cook for 2 minutes. Season stew to taste with salt and pepper.

RICE

Ingredients:

* 2 cups of water
* 1 cup of white, small-grain rice
* 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
* Salt

Directions:

1. In a sufuria over medium heat, bring water to a boil. Add rice, cooking oil and a large pinch of salt.
2. Bring the sufuria to a simmer then lower the heat and cook while covered for 20 minutes or until all the water is absorbed.
3. Remove from the heat and let it sit while covered for 5 minutes then serve with beef stew.

Enjoy!!!
